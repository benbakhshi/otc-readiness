# Phase 5: Ongoing Compliance — OTCID Alternative Reporting Standard

*Compiled July 2026. The web app computes the calendar below from `company.fiscal_year_end` in the master file.*

## Deadlines (calendar anchors)

| Obligation | Deadline |
|---|---|
| Annual report (disclosure statement + FY financials, one document) | **90 days** after fiscal year end |
| Quarterly reports (Q1–Q3) | **45 days** after quarter end |
| **Management Certification** (replaced attorney letter 7/1/2025) | Annually, concurrent with annual report, **no later than 45 days after the annual report due date** (≈ FYE + 135d) |
| Verified Company Profile via OTCIQ | At least **every 6 months**; immediately after a Change in Control |
| Notification of Late Filing | Within **1 business day** of a missed due date → +5 calendar days (quarterly) / +15 (annual) |
| FINRA corporate-action notice (Rule 6490/10b-17): dividends, splits, name changes, mergers | **10 business days** before record date |
| Change in Control | Notify OTC Markets; new application + fee within 20 days |
| DE franchise tax + annual report | **March 1** |

## Disclosure statement contents (OTCID Disclosure Guidelines v5, July 2025)

Cover page: issuer identity/SIC/contacts; period; shares outstanding **now and at prior FYE**; **shell status** checkbox (Rule 405/12b-2/15c2-11) + whether it changed; **Change in Control** checkbox.

1. Name/address + predecessors (5 yrs), standing, suspensions/halts since inception, 12-month corporate events, bankruptcy (5 yrs)
2. Security information per class: symbol, CUSIP, par, authorized, outstanding, holders of record (each "as of"), rights/preferences, modifications
3. **Issuance history — 2 completed fiscal years + interim**, tabular, every change in outstanding shares: date, type, shares, value, discount-to-market flag, recipient (**name control persons of entities**), reason, restricted status, exemption; opening/closing balances. Plus **convertible debt table** (holder, principal, balance w/ accrued interest, maturity, conversion terms)
4. Business, products, services; subsidiaries
5. Facilities
6. Officers/directors/**5%+ owners** table (shares by class, undiluted %, entity control persons; must match the OTC profile)
7. Legal/disciplinary history (10 yrs, six enumerated categories) + pending issuer litigation
8. Third-party providers (counsel, accountant, IR, social channels, disclosure preparers)
9. Financial disclosure: preparer + qualifications; GAAP/IFRS checkbox; statements: balance sheet, income, cash flows, changes in equity, notes (+ audit letter only if audited); **comparative presentation mandatory**; **machine-readable, same document**
10. **Certifications by CEO AND CFO** (reviewed / no material misstatement or omission / fairly presents)

**Staleness:** interims older than 6 months and annuals older than ~15–16 months won't be counted. Financials older than 16 months ⇒ Expert Market risk.

## Falling behind

- Removal from OTCID is **immediate and automatic** on eligibility failure → **Pink Limited** (if financials for a FYE within 16 months are public) or **Expert Market** (no public quoting).
- **Cure:** requalification is automatic once all criteria are met again (no Change in Control); after a Change in Control, reapply with fees.

## Other ongoing duties

- **Material news** promptly via OTC Disclosure & News Service; reflect in next periodic report.
- **Affiliates** must disclose affiliate status to their broker before trading; insiders/10%+ on the verified profile.
- **Blue sky:** OTC's statewide secondary-trading exemptions (41 states OTCQX / 37 OTCQB, Sept 2025) generally do **not** cover OTCID — rely on manual exemptions (Mergent, ~44 jurisdictions) or state-by-state counsel analysis.
- Annual OTC fee — **$8,040/yr** as of July 1, 2026 (verified against the fee schedule); termination for non-payment allows a $1,000 re-activation within 30 days, full reapplication after. DE good standing; respond to OTC Markets inquiries.
