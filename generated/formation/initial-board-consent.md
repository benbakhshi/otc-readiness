**RESOLVED**, that the officers of the Corporation are authorized to open one or more bank accounts in the name of the Corporation and to execute such resolutions and signature cards as such bank may require.

## 7. Employer Identification Number; Qualifications

**RESOLVED**, that the officers are authorized to obtain an Employer Identification Number from the Internal Revenue Service and to qualify the Corporation to do business in any jurisdiction where such qualification is required.

**RESOLVED**, that the officers are authorized to engage a transfer agent, legal counsel, and accountants on behalf of the Corporation, and to take all actions necessary to prepare the Corporation for quotation of its Common Stock on the OTC Markets.
